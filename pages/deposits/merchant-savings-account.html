Merchant Savings Account

KB PRASAC Bank offers all merchants (business owners) with a high interest rate of 4.00% per annum for Merchant Savings Account.

What is even better is that merchants can accept payments for goods or services via KHQR code from all banks in Cambodia and can install KB PRASAC Merchant app to manage payment on their business conveniently anywhere, anytime.
