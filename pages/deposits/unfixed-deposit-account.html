Unfixed Deposit Account

It is another type of savings account offering higher interest rate than normal savings account. You can perform any banking transactions such as withdrawal, fund transfer, Cash-Code or fund transfer to non-account, water, electricity and school bill payments, and mobile top-up with free of charge.

You also receive ATM card and activate KB PRASAC Mobile for managing your cash easily at anytime and anywhere.
